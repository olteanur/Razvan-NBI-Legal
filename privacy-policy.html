This Privacy Policy describes how Razvan NBI ("we", "us", or "our"), operated by North Bucharest Investments, collects, uses, and handles information in connection with our content automation application.

1. Information We Collect

Our application is used exclusively for automated content publishing to social media platforms on behalf of North Bucharest Investments. We may collect or process the following:

  • TikTok account credentials and access tokens (used solely for content publishing)
  • Metadata about published content (post IDs, timestamps, engagement metrics)
  • Technical logs for debugging and performance monitoring

2. How We Use Information

  • To publish real estate investment content to authorized TikTok and Instagram accounts
  • To monitor publishing performance and automate scheduling
  • To improve the reliability of our automation system

We do not sell, rent, or share any collected data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Platforms

Our application interacts with the following third-party platforms:

  • TikTok for Developers — content publishing via TikTok Content Posting API
  • Meta / Instagram Graph API — content publishing to Instagram Business accounts
  • ElevenLabs — AI voice generation for content production
  • HeyGen — AI video generation for content production

Each platform has its own Privacy Policy governing the use of their services.

4. Data Retention

Access tokens are stored securely and used only for the duration required to publish content. Logs are retained for up to 30 days for debugging purposes and then deleted.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security practices including encrypted storage of credentials, restricted access controls, and regular security reviews of our automation pipelines.

6. Your Rights

As this application operates on behalf of a single business entity (North Bucharest Investments), it does not process personal data of end users. For any privacy inquiries, contact us at the address below.
